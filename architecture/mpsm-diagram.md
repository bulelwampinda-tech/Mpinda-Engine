# MPSM Evaluation Flow Diagram

The MPSM model evaluates LLM outputs through a structured pipeline.

User Input
    ↓
Prompt Execution
    ↓
Raw LLM Output
    ↓
Dimension Scoring (0–10 each)
    ├── Accuracy & Fidelity
    ├── Structural Compliance
    ├── Governance Risk
    ├── Analytical Depth
    └── Tone & Mandate Alignment
    ↓
Weighted Arithmetic Calculation
    ↓
Governance Risk Escalation Check
    ↓
Deployment Classification
    ↓
Confidence Tagging
    ↓
Final Evaluation Report
